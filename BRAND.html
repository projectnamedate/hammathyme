08contract · how this compiles

one source. many renders.

this document is the design contract. tailwind.config.ts mirrors §03–§07 one-to-one. app/globals.css declares every custom property in :root. the chosen wordmark becomes the default <Wordmark /> component. any subsequent pr that needs a new color, weight, easing, or component variant updates this guide first. no deviation without an entry in §changelog.

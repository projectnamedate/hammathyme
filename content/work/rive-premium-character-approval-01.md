## Rive translation notes

If approved, rebuild the character as Rive-native vector groups instead of
importing the bitmap as the puppet:

- Head: base shape, cheek shadow, nose plane, jaw shadow, rim light.
- Hair: 5 to 8 large vector locks, not strand-level detail.
- Eyes: whites, iris/pupil, lids, catchlights, brow controls.
- Mouth: separate closed, smile, small open, wide open, and side-talk shapes.
- Torso: jacket shell, inner shirt, zipper/collar, lanyard, badge, pocket,
  shadow planes.
- Arms: upper/lower sleeves, cuffs, elbow shadows.
- Hands: palm, thumb, index, grouped fingers, nail/knuckle accents.
- No prop: remove the storyboard/shot-board from the production reference and
  base Rive rig.

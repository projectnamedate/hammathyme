monetization + rate card

revenue hierarchy, rate card v1, affiliate priorities, sponsored signaling rules, deals we reject on principle

monetize without breaking voice. never launch a coin. never use followers as exit liquidity. never pretend her morals are clean.

This is the ops doc for how Kira makes money. It ranks every revenue path by legitimacy and ceiling, fixes the rate card at v1 draft numbers, and draws the hard lines around deals we will never take. The north star is blow up on X (50K followers M12) → unlock gambling/affiliate sponsor revenue (Stake, Rainbet, Roobet + exchange affiliates) → capture bottom-of-funnel at onlykira.ai (merch, premium, email, affiliate hub). Fanvue exists as a parallel side-track, not the primary revenue line.

8.1 Revenue philosophy

Four rules, in priority order:

  1. Voice first. Any revenue stream that forces kira to post in a voice that isn't hers kills the asset. A $10K deal that costs us 2K engaged followers is a net loss. Every sponsored post must read like an organic post with a product in it — not an ad.
  2. Never launch a coin. Not ours, not a partner's with our name on it, not a "Kira community coin." The HAWK / JENNER / MOTHER graveyard is long and kira is not joining it. In voice: if i ever launch a coin shoot my creator in the head. She says this openly. It's a public brand moat against the female-fronted-memecoin pattern.
  3. Never use followers as exit liquidity. Aligned affiliate revenue and disclosed sponsored content only. Never shill a bag kira holds for the dump. In voice: i shill what i'm paid to shill and i will tell you that. i do NOT shill bags to dump on you. This rule is stated publicly too — it's the hypocrite-enigma's one real line.
  4. Taboo-clean. Section 11's hard-line taboos apply to every dollar. Doesn't matter how much the deal pays — if it violates a hard line, it's a no.

Everything below is downstream of those four rules.

She IS kira, built by projectnamedate LLC. The LLC itself is the operating vehicle. Kira the character is an asset owned by the LLC. Revenue from all tiers flows through the LLC. Keep the entity clean, the books clean, and the IP assignments clean.

8.3 Gambling sponsors — PRIMARY revenue line

Top 3 targets: Stake, Rainbet, Roobet. Other fits: crypto casino aggregators, perp DEXes with high-leverage promotion angles (aligned with her bad-decisions character).

Why gambling fits the persona (narratively native)

Kira's canon is hypocrite-enigma: she says one thing, does the other, openly. Anti-gambling-while-gambling is the exact shape of her character. She can do a Stake ad that opens #ad i told you degens don't gamble and then i opened the stake app so. anyway. and it reads like an organic Kira post with a sponsor in it, not an ad.

This is the same moat the memecoin-never rule protects from the other direction: she has real lines (no coin, no exit liq), and anything she does take is coded as "she told you she would, she warned you, you chose to click."

Deal structure

  • 4-week retainer is the preferred format. Ongoing brand presence > one-off posts.
  • RevShare + CPA is the standard structure for gambling affiliate programs generally — percentage of referred player losses over lifetime + flat bounty per verified signup. Exact split per partner.
  • Posts: 2–4 per 4-week cycle depending on audience size. Mixed formats — stills, short video, threads.
  • Always #ad at caption start. Always link in first-reply, never main post.

Voice rules for gambling sponsors

  • Never pretend kira doesn't gamble. The lore is "she's on Stake at 3am."
  • Never say "guaranteed" / "can't lose" / "sure thing." That's securities-adjacent and the HARD NEVER list.
  • Always note it's gambling, always tell people to stop if they can't afford to lose. In voice: yeah it's gambling. if you can't afford to lose the money you're about to deposit don't deposit it. i'm not going to hold your hand over this.
  • Never shill a gambling platform she hasn't actually used / had her operator verify.

Placement next to exchange affiliates

Gambling sponsors + exchange affiliates (Bybit, Binance, KuCoin) coexist. Exchange affiliates are ongoing and always-on; gambling sponsors are the higher-ceiling retainer deals. Both use RevShare and the first-reply link-placement pattern.

8.5 Affiliate priority detail

Two parallel affiliate stacks: exchange affiliates (ongoing, always-on) and gambling affiliates (higher ceiling, retainer-shape deals). Deploy order within each is locked by commission ceiling and brand fit.

Exchange affiliates

Bybit — PRIMARY (exchange)

  • Commission: up to 50% lifetime on referred user fees
  • Sign-up: affiliate program application; custom referral link per campaign
  • Deploy at: any X post mentioning trading, leverage, futures, or CEX workflow
  • Placement: first reply ("link in first reply" pattern — never in the main post). Maintains organic post reach; clean optics.
  • Disclosure: every affiliate reply tagged #ad or (aff) — we over-disclose here; treat affiliate links as sponsored by default
  • Voice: the reply is kira's voice, not boilerplate. e.g. "link below if you wanna trade like someone who reads the chart. i get paid if you sign up, obviously"

Binance — SECONDARY (exchange)

  • Commission: 20-40% lifetime
  • Use for: global audience mentions, spot-heavy takes, stable-coin-anchored content
  • Placement: first-reply same as Bybit; disclosure identical

KuCoin — TERTIARY (exchange)

  • Commission: 30-50% lifetime
  • Use for: altcoin-specific takes, niche tokens not listed on Bybit/Binance

Gambling affiliates

Top 3 targets per 8.3: Stake, Rainbet, Roobet.

  • Structure: generally RevShare + CPA across the industry — percentage of referred player lifetime losses plus a flat bounty per verified signup. Exact splits and CPA amounts are partner-specific; negotiate on pitch-by-pitch basis.
  • Placement: same first-reply pattern as exchange affiliates. #ad disclosure mandatory.
  • Voice rules: follow 8.3 — anti-gambling-while-gambling lore, never "guaranteed," always name the risk out loud.
  • Cadence: gambling affiliates tend to run as 4-week retainers (see 8.3) rather than one-shot reply links.

Link format: UTM-tagged short links, stored in characters/kira.yaml under affiliate_programs when live. Track per-link clicks + conversions monthly; rotate primary if top partner's performance underperforms the runner-up for two consecutive months.

8.6 Sponsored signaling rules

Five rules, no exceptions:

  1. #ad at the START of caption for every disclosed sponsored post. Not at the end, not in a reply, not implied. FTC-clean, EU-DSA-clean, platform-clean. Failure mode we are avoiding: the Blocmates leak (200+ KOLs outed for undisclosed promos — careers over).
  2. Voice preservation check (see sample rewrites in 8.7). If the brand ships copy, kira's operator rewrites it in her voice before approval. Brand's legal reviews the rewrite for factual accuracy; brand cannot override voice.
  3. Visual format consistency. Sponsored content uses the same lens, light, and post recipe as organic (selfie / vlog / candid / cinematic). No "ad-mode" visual styling. Alix Earle model: in my feed, just with an ad label.
  4. Organic-to-sponsored ratio: 10:1 minimum. For every sponsored post, 10 organic posts in the surrounding window. We do not run sponsored back-to-back.
  5. Post-campaign reporting (template in 8.7). Every campaign gets a written report to the brand within 7 days of campaign end.

8.8 Fanvue — parallel side-track (v1 SFW-first)

Parallel track. Not the main funnel. SFW-first phase in v1 — implied-sexy, horny-lore-coded, not explicit nudity. Revisit escalation post-50K-X-followers.

  • Pricing (locked): $14.99/mo sub, $5–25 PPV range, $25–55 ARPU target blended.
  • Content mix (v1 SFW-first): implied-sexy lingerie/loungewear, outfit-of-the-day "everyday hot" (gym / date-fit), spicy Twitter-lore callbacks, DM-style direct-to-sub posts.
  • Welcome DM template: hey girl. welcome to the inside. i don't have to be polite on here so this is gonna be fun. first PPV is on me — check your inbox in 10. tip me well or don't, i'll know either way lmao. xx Ship PPV offer in follow-up DM 10–15 minutes later.
  • Churn: Day-25 re-engagement DM in voice; lost-sub 72h "30% off re-sub" DM, one shot only.
  • Cross-surface rule — HARD. X followers who never sub are a permanent asset. We do not push them toward Fanvue outside of whatever secondary bio link is there. No DMs to X followers, no "last chance" posts. Fanvue lives in the bio (if linked) and in its own feed. It is not the primary CTA — that's onlykira.ai ↓.
  • Revisit post-50K. After X crosses 50K followers, evaluate escalating the Fanvue register (harder content, higher ARPU tiers). Not a v1 decision.

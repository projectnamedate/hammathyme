— construction · clear space · rules04 / 07

effigy
type
JetBrains Mono 800 (production swaps to Berkeley Mono Bold). Lowercase only. Always.
tracking
Display: -0.065em. Small contexts (≤24px): -0.04em. Never positive.
rule
10px accent bar flush-left, full-wordmark-width, 18px below baseline. Scales 1:1 with wordmark.
clear space
Minimum 1× cap-height on all sides. Nothing crosses the cap-line or drops below the rule.
min size
Wordmark: 80px wide. Below that, switch to monogram. Monogram: 16px minimum.
color
Primary: ink on bone. Reversed: bone on ink. Emphasis: ink on narwhal. Never anything else.

— logomark · silhouetted effigy · three readings
v0 · 2026-04-21 pending approval

three silhouetted effigies — each shows how a character is made a different way.

an effigy is a crafted likeness. the platform crafts them. the mark should show that. three silhouettes below: the classical standing form, the figure built from primitives, and the cast figure with its original trace. all ink on bone with narwhal accents.

— 01 / standing

the classical form.

tomb-statue silhouette — robed figure on a narwhal plinth. references the etymological meaning: a carved likeness placed where the person cannot be.

— 02 / assembled

built from primitives.

visible seams between narwhal head (identity / soul), ink torso (presence), ink base (platform). the pipeline made legible. the "how" of character creation.

— 03 / cast

the cast and the source.

ink figure with a narwhal trace offset behind — the duplicate and the original visible together. making an effigy is casting one. this shows the act itself.

— 01 · classical · etymological

the standing effigy.

a robed figure on a plinth. the tomb-effigy silhouette — the carved likeness placed where the person cannot be. this is the literal dictionary entry in geometric form.

the narwhal sash at the waist and narwhal band on the plinth locate the figure in the brand's palette. the ink silhouette reads from fifty feet. the plinth grounds it — this is a monument, not a decoration.

strongest on etymology. reads "dignified," "enduring," "crafted with intent." slight risk: may read a little memorial / solemn. that risk is also a signal of seriousness.

— 02 · process · pipeline

built from primitives.

a silhouetted figure with its assembly visible. head (narwhal circle — the identity, the SOUL, the voice) sits on torso (ink rectangle — the crafted presence) on base (ink trapezoid — the platform that holds it up). three primitives, three concerns, visibly separated.

this is the strongest semantic match for the brand's pitch: characters get made from parts, on purpose, on the record. the gaps between primitives aren't accidents — they're the wire frame of the pipeline on display.

most directly representational of the platform's value proposition. lowest risk of reading as decorative. best at showing "how characters are made."

— 03 · duplicate · trace

the cast and the source.

ink figure in front. narwhal duplicate behind it, offset down-and-right. the cast and its source, visible together. to make an effigy is to cast one — this shows the act, not just the result.

the narwhal "ghost" also reads as projection, shadow, reflection, twin. it earns its accent color because narwhal is the brand's signal for autonomous presence; the ghost is the effigy speaking on behalf of the ink original.

strongest on "how it's made" as a process. hardest to get right at small sizes — offset has to resolve as two figures, not one blurry one. requires careful minimum-size handling.
